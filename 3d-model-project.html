3D Modeled Tape Dispenser

A playful sea otter-inspired design rendered in Blender and optimized for 3D printing.

Project Overview

This 3D modeled tape dispenser takes inspiration from the way sea otters hold objects while floating on their backs. I modeled it in Blender with an emphasis on a functional, ergonomic, and joyful design that looks great on a modern desk.

Design Highlights

  • Ergonomic shape mimicking sea otter posture
  • Blender-modeled with smooth organic sculpting
  • 3D printable with supports considered
  • Non-bottom blade for top-access cutting

Tools Used

  • Blender 3.6 (Modeling & Rendering)
  • Cycles Renderer
  • Adobe Photoshop (for final image touch-ups)
